Healthy Porridge Bowl

Start your day right with this filling bowl of oats, berries, banana and seeds. It's healthy and packed with nutrients to fuel body and mind.

Prep time:

Cooking time:

Total time:

Servings:

10 mins

5 mins

15 mins

2

Ingredients
  • 100g frozen raspberries
  • 1 orange, ½ sliced and ½ juiced
  • 150g porridge oats
  • 100ml milk
  • ½ banana, sliced
  • 2 tbsp smooth almond butter
  • 1 tbsp goji berries
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
Instructions
  1. Tip half the raspberries and all of the orange juice in a pan.
  2. Simmer until the raspberries soften, about 5 mins.
  3. Meanwhile stir the oats, milk and 450ml water in a pan over a low heat until creamy.
  4. Top with the raspberry compote, remaining raspberries, orange slices, banana, almond butter, goji berries and chia seeds.

Mushroom hash with poached eggs

Get three of your 5-a-day before midday with this nutritious recipe. Mushrooms are a good source of zinc, a key nutrient for maintaining healthy skin.

Prep time:

Cooking time:

Total time:

Servings:

10 mins

17 mins

27 mins

2

Ingredients
  • 1 ½ tbsp avocado oil
  • 2 large onions, halved and sliced
  • 150g porridge oats
  • 100ml milk
  • 500g closed cup mushrooms, quartered
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme leaves, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 500g fresh tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 4 tsp omega seed mix
  • 4 large eggs
Instructions
  1. Heat the oil in a large non-stick frying pan and fry the onions for a few mins.
  2. Cover the pan and leave the onions to cook in their own steam for 5 mins more.
  3. Tip in the mushrooms with the thyme and cook, stirring frequently, for 5 mins until softened.
  4. Add the tomatoes and paprika, cover the pan and cook for 5 mins until pulpy.
  5. Stir through the seed mix.
  6. If you're making this recipe as part of our two-person Summer Healthy Diet Plan, poach two of the eggs in lightly simmering water to your liking.
  7. Serve on top of half the hash with a sprinkling of fresh thyme and some black pepper.
  8. Chill the remaining hash to warm in a pan and eat with freshly poached eggs on another day.
  9. If you're serving four people, poach all four eggs, divide the hash between four plates, sprinkle with thyme and black pepper and serve with the eggs on top.

Breakfast Burrito

Fill a nutritious wholemeal wrap with lots of healthy breakfast ingredients to make this veggie burrito. We’ve included protein-rich eggs and avocado to add good fats.

Prep time:

Cooking time:

Total time:

Servings:

5 mins

5 mins

10 mins

1

Ingredients
  • 1 tsp chipotle paste
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp rapeseed oil
  • 50g kale
  • 7 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ small avocado, sliced
  • 1 wholemeal tortilla wrap, warmed
Instructions
  1. Whisk the chipotle paste with the egg and some seasoning in a jug.
  2. Heat the oil in a large frying pan, add the kale and tomatoes.
  3. Cook until the kale is wilted and the tomatoes have softened, then push everything to the side of the pan.
  4. Pour the beaten egg into the cleared half of the pan and scramble.
  5. Layer everything into the centre of your wrap, topping with the avocado, then wrap up and eat immediately.
